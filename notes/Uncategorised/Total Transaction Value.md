# Total Transaction Value

## Dependencies
This is the full amount of all transactions that flow through a `platform` or a `marketplace`. So you might have a platform that lists hotel rooms and then sells them to customers. The hotel rooms price is not your revenue but you do get a `Revenue Margin` or `Take Rate` of this.

I found this first looking at `WEB Travel Group`.

## Dependents
This is tied into `EDITDA/TTV` ratios which allows investors to see how profitable the company is when they scale up the business.
