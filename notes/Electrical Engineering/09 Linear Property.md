# Dependencies
This is based on `linearity` where the cause and effect have a linear relationship. This property is based on `homogeneity` property, also known as `scaling` and `additivity` property.

So for a resistor. We have $v=iR$ which can be scaled by a constant *k*.

The additivity property says that the sum of inputs is the sum of the outputs.

Where:

$$
v_1 = i_1 R \\
v_2 = i_2 R \\
v_\text{total} = (i_1 + i_2)R = v_1 + v_2
$$
