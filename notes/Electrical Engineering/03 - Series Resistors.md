# Series Resistors

# Dependencies
The equivalent resistance of that resistors in series is just a summation.


$$
R_{eq} = \sum_{n=1}^{N} R_n
$$

To calculate individual voltages:

$$
v_1 = \frac{R_1}{R_1 + R2} v_2
$$
