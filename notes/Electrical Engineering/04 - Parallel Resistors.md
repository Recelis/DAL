# Dependencies
The equivalent resistance of that resistors in parallel is the product of the resistors divided by their sum.


$$
R_{eq} = \frac{R_1R_2}{R_1 + R_2} 
$$

To calculate individual currents through each resistor:

You get the other resistances and divided by the sum.

$$
i_1 = \frac{R_2}{R_1 + R2} i
$$
